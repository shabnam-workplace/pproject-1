About the Program

Apex Planet offers internship opportunities in web and app development. These internships are remote and can be for 30 or 45 days. The company focuses on professional software, technology, and blogs. They are looking for interns in various fields, including web development with PHP & MySQL, app development, and web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as fresher trainee roles.
